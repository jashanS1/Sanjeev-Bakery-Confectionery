About Sanjeev Bakery

A Trusted Name in Ludhiana

Sanjeev Bakery & Confectionery is an established, trusted local bakery located at Ajit Nagar, Ludhiana. We believe in one simple philosophy: baking fresh, delivering happiness.

For years, we have been serving our community with the finest quality cakes, crispy snacks, buttery cookies, and premium confectionery. Whether you need a lavish designer cake for a birthday or just a fresh paneer patty for your evening tea, we pour our heart into every recipe.
